At Tri Tec EV LLC, we are at the forefront of driving the global shift towards sustainability with our comprehensive range of electric vehicles and advanced technologies. From industrial solutions like forklifts and sanitation trucks to electric 2-wheelers and public transport vehicles, our portfolio is designed to meet the diverse needs of modern businesses. We are dedicated to providing not just products, but complete solutions that include after-sales services, spare parts, and charging infrastructure. Our mission is to empower companies worldwide with the tools and support they need to transition to greener, more efficient operations, paving the way for a sustainable future in every sector we serve.

Our Vision

To create a world where electric mobility is the norm, not the exception. We aim to provide a robust and innovative platform that accelerates the global shift toward sustainable transportation, empowering businesses and communities to embrace cleaner, greener technologies. Through our commitment to excellence and environmental stewardship, we envision a future where our solutions lead the way to a healthier planet and a thriving, sustainable economy.

Our Mission

To empower businesses and innovators by providing them with the cutting-edge tools, resources, and unwavering support needed to drive the global transition to electric mobility. We are committed to building a strong foundation for a future where electric vehicles dominate the transportation landscape. By fostering innovation, supporting sustainable practices, and delivering comprehensive solutions, we aim to enable our partners to lead this transformative journey, making electric vehicles accessible, reliable, and indispensable across all sectors. Our mission is to ensure that electric mobility becomes the standard, driving progress towards a cleaner, more sustainable world for generations to come.
